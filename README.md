# Customer-Behavior-Data-Analyst



Designed an interactive Power BI dashboard to analyze customer behavior, sales performance, and revenue trends.

Analyzed 4,000+ customer records to identify purchasing patterns across gender, age groups, and subscription status.

Built key KPIs including Average Review Rating (3.75), Total Customers (4K), and Average Purchase Value ($59.76).

Visualized revenue by product category, highlighting Clothing as the top-performing segment.

Performed age-group analysis to identify Young Adults as the highest revenue-generating and most active customer group.

Implemented dynamic slicers for Gender, Subscription Status, Shipping Type, and Category for real-time filtering.

Created donut and bar charts to compare subscribed vs non-subscribed customers (27% vs 73%).

Delivered actionable business insights to support marketing strategies, customer retention, and revenue optimization.

Applied data modeling and DAX measures to ensure accurate calculations and smooth interactivity.

Focused on clean UI design and usability, making insights easy to understand for non-technical stakeholders.
